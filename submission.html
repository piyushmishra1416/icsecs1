Submission

Author guidelines
Paper Submission:

  • Authors are invited to submit full papers in standard IEEE double-column format.

  • Email submissions will NOT be accepted.

  • Authors should only submit original work that has neither appeared elsewhere for publication, nor is under review for another refereed publication.

  • To enable blind review, the author list should be omitted i.e. do not include author name and affiliation in the paper.

  • All submitted papers will be checked for plagiarism through the IEEE CrossCheck system.

  • Plagiarism should be below 20%.

  • Papers with significant overlap with the authors own papers or other papers will be rejected without review.

  • Authors of the accepted papers will be informed by email.

  • Authors of accepted papers should send the camera-ready paper in the word format and IEEE PDFXPRESS compatible pdf format along with the transaction details to ieeespices2024@iiitkottayam.ac.in on or before July 24, 2024.

Manuscript Template:

  • Authors are strongly encouraged to use the latest templates for typesetting as provided on the IEEE Website.

  • Maximum page limit of a paper is 6 pages in 2 column IEEE Conference paper style.

  • Click on the link, for more details about manuscrip template ....
Paper Template

Paper Publication:

  • Every accepted paper MUST have at least one author registered to the conference and at least one of the authors is also expected to attend the conference and present the paper.

  • Papers not presented at the conference will not be included in the final program or in the digital proceedings.

  • All registered and presented papers will be submitted to IEEE Xplore for possible publication.

How to submit your paper?
  • The manuscript as a single PDF is to be submitted online through Microsoft CMT .

IEEE SPICES 2024

IEEE International Conference on Signal Processing, Informatics, Communication and Energy Systems 2024 (IEEE SPICES 2024) is a flagship conference of IEEE Kerala Section jointly organized by IIIT Kottayam during 20-22 September 2024. The theme of the conference is “Harmonizing Signals, Data, and Energy: Bridging the Digital Future”. It is a platform for technical exchange amongst researchers from academia, research laboratories, and industries in various emerging fields of Signal Processing, Communication, Computer Science, Energy Systems, Instrumentation & Control Systems, Robotics and Smart Cities and so on. The technical program includes keynote speeches, plenary talks, regular technical sessions, and special sessions.
